<p><p>Fiberglass Reinforced Plastics (FRP) are composite materials combining fiberglass with plastic resins, enhancing strength and durability while reducing weight. The FRP market is primarily categorized into thermosetting and thermoplastic products. Thermosetting plastics, such as epoxy and polyester, harden permanently after curing, offering excellent chemical resistance and structural integrity. In contrast, thermoplastic products can be reshaped upon heating, providing greater flexibility and ease of processing. Both types are widely used in construction, automotive, aerospace, and electrical applications due to their performance benefits.</p></p>
