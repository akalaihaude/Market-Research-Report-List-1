<p><strong>The Drilling Head Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diamond Drilling Head</li><li>Cone Drilling Head</li><li>Drag Drilling Head</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drilling head market consists of various types, each suited for specific applications. Diamond drilling heads use diamond-tipped bits for precision and efficiency, ideal for hard rock formations. Cone drilling heads feature conical bits that excel in soft to medium formations, providing effective cutting and stability. Drag drilling heads are designed for loose or unconsolidated materials, utilizing a flat blade to scrape and cut. Together, these drilling head types cater to diverse geological conditions and operational requirements in the drilling industry.</p></p>
