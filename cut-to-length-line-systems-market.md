<p><p>Cut-to-length line systems are categorized into three market types: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic. Manual systems require operators to handle all processes, relying heavily on human labor for cutting materials to specified lengths. Semi-automatic systems involve a combination of machinery and human intervention, where operators assist in feeding materials while the machine performs cutting tasks. Automatic systems operate independently, utilizing advanced technology to manage the entire cutting process with minimal human input, enhancing efficiency and precision in production.</p></p>
