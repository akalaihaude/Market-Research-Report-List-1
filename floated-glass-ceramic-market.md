<p><p>The floated glass-ceramic market includes two primary types: laminated glass and filmed glass. Laminated glass consists of multiple glass layers bonded together with a transparent interlayer, enhancing safety and reducing UV exposure. It's widely used in architecture and automotive applications. Filmed glass, on the other hand, involves applying a thin film to standard glass, which can provide additional thermal insulation, UV protection, or aesthetic enhancements. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs, driving innovation and market growth.</p></p>
