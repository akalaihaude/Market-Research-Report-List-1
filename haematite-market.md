<p><p>The haematite market can be categorized into three main types: lump, pellet, and powder. Lump haematite consists of larger, naturally occurring chunks, commonly used in iron ore production. Pellets are small, round balls formed by agglomerating iron ore fines, improving the efficiency of steel production through enhanced uniformity and reduced impurities. Powdered haematite, finely crushed and ground, finds applications in various industries, including pigments and coatings. Each type serves distinct purposes, impacting both production methods and market demand.</p></p>
