<p><p>The mask repair machine market comprises three key technologies: </p><p>1. **Laser Technology** utilizes high-intensity lasers to precisely remove defects from photomasks, ensuring high accuracy and minimal damage to the substrate. </p><p>2. **Focused Ion Beam (FIB) Technology** employs ion beams to mill and repair mask defects at the nanoscale, offering exceptional precision for critical applications. </p><p>3. **Nanomachining Technology** involves advanced techniques to manipulate materials at the atomic level, enabling fine-tuning of mask features for enhanced performance in semiconductor manufacturing. Each technology serves specialized needs within the industry.</p></p>
