<p><p>The Piezoelectric Tile market comprises two main types: hard ceramics and soft ceramics. Hard ceramics, typically composed of materials like zirconate and titanate, exhibit high mechanical strength and stability, making them suitable for applications requiring durability. Soft ceramics, on the other hand, have lower stiffness and are generally better at converting mechanical stress into electrical energy, enhancing sensitivity. Both types serve various markets, including energy harvesting and sensor technologies, catering to diverse industrial and consumer needs.</p></p>
