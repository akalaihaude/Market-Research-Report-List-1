<p><p>The waste paper balers market comprises two primary types: horizontal and vertical balers. Horizontal balers are designed for high-volume processing, efficiently compressing and packaging waste paper into dense bales for easy handling and transportation. They are ideal for large facilities with continuous operations. In contrast, vertical balers are compact, suitable for smaller operations or locations with limited space. They compress waste paper vertically, requiring manual loading, making them a cost-effective choice for businesses with lower volumes. Both types enhance recycling and waste management efficiency.</p></p>
