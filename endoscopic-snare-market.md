<p><p>An Endoscopic Snare is a specialized medical instrument used during endoscopic procedures to capture and remove tissue, such as polyps or tumors, from within the body. The versatility and effectiveness of endoscopic snares make them crucial in minimally invasive surgeries, leading to shorter recovery times and reduced patient discomfort.</p><p>The Endoscopic Snare Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.9% during the forecast period, driven by factors such as increasing prevalence of gastrointestinal disorders, a rising geriatric population, advancements in minimally invasive surgical techniques, and growing awareness of the benefits of endoscopic procedures.
